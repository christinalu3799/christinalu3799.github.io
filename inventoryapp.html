Inspiration

From my experience of working in the restaurant industry, I've noticed the headache and frustration that my colleagues often face when doing inventory every week:

Tiny rows and columns listing dozens of items printed on a wrinkly sheet of paper and slapped onto a clipboard.

Knowing there had to be a way to make this process less painful, I created this simple application to streamline the inventory process.

Technologies Used

  • Express
  • Node.js
  • EJS
  • Mongoose
